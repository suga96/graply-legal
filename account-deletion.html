Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your Jiujify account and all the personal data associated with it. We offer two ways to request deletion: directly inside the app (instant) or by email.

App Information

  • App name: Jiujify — The Jiu-Jitsu App
  • Developer: Jiujify
  • Package name: com.bjjtracker.bjj_app
  • Contact: Jiujify@gmail.com

Option 1: Delete from inside the app (recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the app. The deletion is processed immediately:

  1. Open Jiujify on your device.
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom navigation).
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by typing the requested word and tapping the final delete button.

Your account, training history, and all associated personal data are removed immediately upon confirmation. You will be signed out and the account cannot be recovered.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app (for example, you uninstalled it, lost access to your device, or are unable to sign in), send us an email request:

To: Jiujify@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Body: Include the email address used to register the Jiujify account you want deleted. To prevent unauthorized requests, you must send the email from that same address.

We process email-based deletion requests within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email when your data has been deleted.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account (via either option), the following personal data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account credentials (email, password hash, authentication tokens)
  • Your profile information (name, belt rank, stripes, academy, profile photo)
  • All training sessions you have logged (date, duration, type, notes, ratings)
  • Your technique catalog progress and saved technique data
  • Your goals, streaks, and progress history
  • Notification preferences and scheduled reminders
  • Any user-generated content (notes, custom lists, etc.)

What is retained, and for how long

Some limited data may be retained after account deletion. This is either required by law, necessary for legitimate operational reasons, or fully anonymized so it cannot identify you:

  • Anonymized usage analytics — aggregated metrics that cannot be linked back to you. Retained indefinitely for product improvement.
  • Subscription & payment records — if you ever subscribed to Jiujify Premium, the receipt and transaction history are held by the payment platform you used (Apple App Store, Google Play, or RevenueCat). These records are retained according to each platform's own policies and applicable tax/accounting regulations (typically up to 7 years). We do not store credit card or bank account details ourselves.
  • Logs & backups — routine system backups and security logs may retain references to deleted records for up to 30 days, after which they are purged automatically.

If you need a payment record purged, you must contact the corresponding payment platform directly (Apple, Google, or RevenueCat).

Cancelling your subscription

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active Jiujify Premium subscription. Subscriptions are managed by the platform where you purchased them. To avoid future charges, cancel through:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions

We recommend cancelling your subscription before deleting your account.

Questions or problems

If you have any difficulty deleting your account, did not receive a confirmation, or have questions about how your data is handled, contact us at Jiujify@gmail.com. For full details on data handling, see our Privacy Policy.
